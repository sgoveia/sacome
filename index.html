Introduction

Welcome!!!

spacetimeAthleta is a HaaS(Hardware as a Service)/SaaS(Software as a Service) platform, providing real-time biometric sensing, tracking, predictive analytics and performance-intelligence for athletic teams.

Utilizing the latest in cellular connectivity, bio-sensors, GPS, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

As a coach, trainer, manager and athlete you can interface with the platform through any connected device(Android, iPhone, Desktop) in real-time from anywhere during an event. Unique in the industry the spacetimeAthleta platform can scale to an unlimted number of athletes and teams, all in an unparalleled cost saving structure.

We think every team should have access to our platform, from tiny highschools to professional teams.

We answer questions like:

  • Through analytics and machine learning, how can we maximize athletic performance as a team and as individual players?
  • How do we know if our athletes are overtrained?
  • How can we tell if a player is under utilized or not performing to thier physical potiental?
  • How can we make predictions on performance?
  • At the current state of wellness and environmental factors, is heat exhaustion potientially imminent?
  • How does body temperature, heart rate, speed and distance covered correlate to performance?
  • Who should really sit out this quarter vs. the next based on analysis and not "gut"?
  • And.. most importantly, how can we do all this in real-time during a game, practice or event?

We are passionate about delivering the most seemless, cost enabling, and advanced athlete performance-intelligence platform for your team!!

Currently we are in the midst of hardware design and core software architecture development. Our plan is to internally release an Alpha build by Spring 2018 with hardware prototypes to follow in late summer. We also plan to begin interviews with coaches and trainers to get their input from the onset. Stay tuned for something inspiring!

Please send us a message, we want to talk!

Sincerely,
spacetimeAthleta Team
